- autonomous deployment to production;
- financial transactions or trading execution;
- legal, medical, or employment decisions about a person;
- access to credentials, private keys, or unrestricted customer data;
- destructive repository or infrastructure actions;
- vulnerability exploitation outside an explicitly authorized environment;
- claims that cannot be reviewed by a qualified human;
- a participant under pressure to buy or upgrade a plan.
